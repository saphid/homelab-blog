Historical context

How the systems got here

The archived Codex sessions still matter, but they are now treated as build history rather than present-tense truth.

  • The kitchen screen started as a fridge-dashboard Flask app and later moved to a Next.js monorepo.
  • The Orange Pi moved from SD boot to eMMC and from “competition experiment” to real monitoring node.
  • The Termux phone shifted from OpenClaw ambitions to a practical Android sensor and HA bridge.
